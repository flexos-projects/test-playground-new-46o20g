<flex_block type="instructions">
Vision specs capture the project's identity and direction.
These are often small — a paragraph or two — capturing things the user
says in passing that define who they are, what they want, and what matters.

SUBTYPES:
- goal: What the project aims to achieve. Mission, objectives, success metrics.
- constraint: Hard limits. Budget, timeline, regulatory, accessibility requirements.
- audience: Who uses this. Personas, demographics, user segments.
- brand: Identity, voice, personality, visual direction (not specific tokens — those go in design/).

NAMING: NNN-{subtype}-{slug}.md
EXAMPLE: 001-goal-rescue-mission.md, 002-audience-dog-adopters.md

STATUS: roadmap (planned), active (current), archived (no longer relevant).

Each file should have:
- Clear title
- Type: vision, subtype: one of the four above
- Prose explaining the vision point
- Optional tags for cross-referencing
</flex_block>
