# A&E Doctors

The primary users are doctors working in the Accident & Emergency department. They are typically under significant time pressure and deal with a wide range of patient conditions, from minor injuries to critical life-threatening emergencies. Their workflow requires rapid access to patient information, diagnostic tools, and communication features. Existing tools are perceived as clunky, slow, or difficult to use, leading to frustration and potential delays in patient care. This app aims to streamline their tasks and reduce friction in their daily responsibilities.
