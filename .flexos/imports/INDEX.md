<flex_block type="instructions">
Landing zone for raw imported content. Files here are temporary.

When a user imports a website, PDF, document, or other content,
it lands here first. The AI then processes it:
1. Extract relevant information
2. Create proper specs in the right folders
3. Move or archive the raw import

Files here can be any format. They should be triaged promptly —
this folder should not accumulate permanently.

NAMING: NNN-{slug}.md
EXAMPLE: 001-old-website-scrape.md, 002-client-brief.md
</flex_block>
