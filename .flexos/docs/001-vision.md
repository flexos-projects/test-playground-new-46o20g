# Vision & Strategy

## Goals

### 001-goal-improve-a-and-e-doctor-experience.md
**Improve A&E Doctor Experience**
Create a highly usable and efficient app that addresses the pain points of existing A&E doctor applications. The core purpose of this application is to provide A&E doctors with a tool that significantly enhances their efficiency, reduces cognitive load, and improves overall job satisfaction by eliminating the frustrations associated with current, poorly designed applications. The app aims to be intuitive, fast, and reliable, allowing doctors to focus on patient care.

## Audience

### 002-audience-a-and-e-doctors.md
**A&E Doctors**
Emergency department doctors who require fast, reliable, and intuitive tools for patient management. The primary users are doctors working in the Accident & Emergency department. They are typically under significant time pressure and deal with a wide range of patient conditions, from minor injuries to critical life-threatening emergencies. Their workflow requires rapid access to patient information, diagnostic tools, and communication features. Existing tools are perceived as clunky, slow, or difficult to use, leading to frustration and potential delays in patient care. This app aims to streamline their tasks and reduce friction in their daily responsibilities.
